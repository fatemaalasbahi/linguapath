# LinguaPath
## AI-Powered Language Proficiency Coach


# Project Overview

LinguaPath is an AI-powered personalized language proficiency coaching platform designed to help students achieve their target scores in language exams.

The platform helps students prepare for exams such as:

- Duolingo English Test (DET)
- HSK Chinese Proficiency Test

LinguaPath evaluates a student's current abilities, identifies weaknesses, creates personalized study plans, provides AI-generated feedback, and tracks progress toward their goals.


# Problem Statement

Many international students need language proficiency exams for:

- university admission
- immigration requirements
- scholarships
- professional opportunities

However, many students struggle because they do not know:

- their current ability level
- what skills they need to improve
- how to organize their study schedule
- whether their preparation is effective

Most existing study resources provide general materials but lack personalized guidance.


# Proposed Solution

LinguaPath acts as a personal AI language exam coach.

The platform will:

1. Evaluate the student's current proficiency level.
2. Identify strengths and weaknesses.
3. Generate a personalized learning roadmap.
4. Provide AI feedback on practice tasks.
5. Track improvement over time.


# Target Users

Primary users:

- International students
- University applicants
- Students preparing for proficiency exams
- Immigrants requiring language certification
